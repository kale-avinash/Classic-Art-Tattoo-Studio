About Us

Ink & Art Studio by Akshay Tattoos and Design Academy

Welcome to our creative studio, located in the heart of Sangamner at Bus Stand Complex. Founded by Akshay Daunde, we specialize in a variety of artistic services including permanent and temporary tattoos, portrait sketching, wall painting, and interior design

Our studio is also a hub for learning, offering drawing classes, calligraphy classes, and tattoo training. With a passion for creativity and detail, we also design unique wallpapers that transform spaces.
